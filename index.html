Why Strong Passwords Matter

A strong password is your first line of defense against hackers and identity theft. Weak passwords like "password123" or "123456" can be cracked in seconds, putting your personal data at risk.

  • Length: Longer passwords (12+ characters) are harder to crack.
  • Variety: Mixing uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols increases complexity.
  • Uniqueness: Use a different password for every account to limit damage if one is compromised.
  • Security: Strong passwords protect your accounts, finances, and privacy from cyber threats.
